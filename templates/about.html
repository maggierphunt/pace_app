Designed to suit your running needs, this app will generate a Spotify playlist to help you complete your run in your goal time.

Provide three separate data points on your usual pace, and discover the cadence you need to run at to achieve your goal.

The app draws on your Spotify library to then pull a playlist together of songs which run at the same beats per minute as you need to make steps per minute - so, simply keep in step, and you'll complete your distance in your goal time!
